About me

I am a fourth year Physics PhD student at the University of California, Davis. I am part of Prof. Maruša Bradač's research group. I use a phenomenon called gravitational lensing to leverage the world's most powerful space and ground-based telescopes to study the first galaxies in the universe. My work was recently featured in a press release from Keck Observatory. See my research page for more information.
